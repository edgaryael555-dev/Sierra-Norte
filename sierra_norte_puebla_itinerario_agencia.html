Sierra Norte de Puebla

A polished, agency-style travel presentation for a family weekend between Huauchinango, Cerro Zempoaltépetl, Cascada La Morena, Piedras Encimadas and Zacatlán.

3 daysSaturday to Monday
2 nightsCabaña Alpina 3

Trip concept

Mountain air, waterfalls and a slow cabin night.

The base itinerary is active but doable: drive early, focus on one main adventure per half-day, keep meals flexible, and protect rest time at the cabin. Sunday is the most demanding day because of the pre-dawn Zempoaltépetl hike.

Decision guide

Keep, cut or swap

The full route is ambitious. This is the simplest decision logic to keep the trip enjoyable.

Must keep

La Morena, one Zempoaltépetl sunrise attempt, slow cabin night and Piedras Encimadas.

Optional if tired

Canal Tlalcoyunga and the full Brujas + Xopanatl route. Do only one if Sunday feels too heavy.

Rain backup

Huauchinango center, coffee/snacks, Presa Tejocotal, cabin rest, or Zacatlán cultural stop.
